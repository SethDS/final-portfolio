About

I like leather boots, watches and peanut butter chocolate ice cream. I think that more of the world's problems could be solved by if statements. My Codewars solutions may not always be the prettiest, but I still feel like I just scored the game winning touchdown when they work. I think that the most important skill a coder can have is the ability to learn, fast. I like Angular better than React. Everyone else says they love a challenge, and so do I. I'm probably not really a extrovert, but I actually kinda like public speaking. Google Maps is my favorite API. My goal is to get my JavaScript as fluent as my Malay. I love Thai food, ginger beer, Sashimi and have read pretty much every Louis L'Amour book. You haven't lived until you've eaten street food in Penang. I'm a programmer, a cook and a musician...and it all feels connected to me.
